Terms of Service

1. Administrator Responsibilities

As an administrator, you are responsible for maintaining platform security, managing user accounts, and ensuring system integrity.

2. Access Control

You will have access to sensitive system data including user information, log files, and security rules. Use this access responsibly and only for legitimate administrative purposes.

3. Confidentiality

You agree to maintain strict confidentiality of all system data, user information, and security configurations. Do not share sensitive data with unauthorized individuals.

4. Compliance with Security Policies

You must comply with all organizational security policies, data protection regulations, and industry standards while using the admin panel.

5. Account Security

You are responsible for maintaining the security of your admin account. Use strong passwords and do not share your login credentials.

6. Audit and Monitoring

All administrator actions are logged and may be audited. Unauthorized or malicious activities will result in immediate account termination.

7. Termination of Access

Your admin access may be suspended or terminated if you violate these terms, or when you no longer require administrative privileges.

8. Changes to Terms

These terms may be updated from time to time. Continued use of admin privileges constitutes acceptance of updated terms.

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect from Admins

We collect the following administrator information:

  • Employee ID: Your unique employee identifier
  • Contact Information: Full name, email address, username
  • Department and Role: Your department and admin level
  • Activity Logs: All actions performed in the admin panel

2. How We Use Admin Information

Your information is used to:

  • Verify your administrator identity and authorization
  • Provide access to admin panel features
  • Audit and monitor system activities
  • Communicate important security updates
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. Data Security for Admin Accounts

Admin accounts are protected with enhanced security measures including:

  • Strong password requirements
  • Session management and timeouts
  • Activity logging and monitoring
  • Secure encrypted storage of credentials

4. Retention of Admin Data

We retain admin account information for as long as you maintain administrator privileges. Activity logs are retained for audit purposes.

5. Access to User Data

As an administrator, you will have access to user data. You are required to handle this data in compliance with our privacy policy and applicable laws.

6. Breach Notification

In the event of a security breach involving admin accounts, affected administrators will be notified promptly.

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at privacy@lumen.com
